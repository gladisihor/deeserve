IT Support

Your business doesn’t run itself; and neither do its IT systems. From email to Internet, your back-up server to your phone system, you need technology to run smoothly so your business can do the same.

IT Support

We offer a comprehensive range of services, packaged for a fixed monthly cost according to your unique needs. It all starts with a free, no-strings IT audit.

  • Remote IT Helpdesk

    From printer issues to Microsoft Office queries, we’re just a phone call away, minimising staff downtime and keeping your business running at full capacity.

  • Server Support

    We can install, maintain and/or upgrade your server(s). We also proactively monitor them for you, ensuring everything is running as it should day and night.

  • Hardware and Software Support

    As official partners of technology heavyweights like HP and Microsoft, we can advise on, install and maintain software solutions designed around your business.

  • Network Support

    We can design, install, set up and/or upgrade your network keeping you communicating by phone, email and internet. Your network should facilitate rather than hamper your communications.

  • Disaster Recovery

    We can help you plan for and manage unforeseen circumstances keeping the most important elements of your business up and running in the event of a disaster such as a fire or massive power outage.

  • IT Equipment

    If you’re not sure what equipment you need at a minimum, we can advise. Then we’ll set it up, configure and maintain it for you from your laptops, PCs and Macs to printers and mobile devices.

  • Advanced Monitoring

    deeserve’s systems are designed to be inherently problem-free. And if anything slows down your flow of traffic or breaks important links, we fix them before they impact your day-to-day operations. Even in calm waters, we remain diligent, avoiding any storms ahead.

  • Backup & Cloud Storage

    If we asked you to put a price on your data and documentation, you’d probably struggle. We’re the back-up equivalent of your own personal Tower of London keeping your most valuable assets safe and secure at all times.
